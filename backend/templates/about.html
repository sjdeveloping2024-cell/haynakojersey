📚 About Pick-A-Book

Pick-A-Book is a simple and efficient library management system built to help librarians manage books, students, and borrowing records with ease.

Version: 2.0.0

Features:

• Book inventory management with add/delete functionality
• Student registration and management
• Borrow and return tracking with due dates
• Dashboard with real-time statistics
• Librarian accounts with secure login

Built with Flask (Python) and SQLite for the backend, with a clean HTML/CSS frontend.
